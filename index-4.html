03 Aug 2021 News

The MGA has no connection with 19msia.com

The Malta Gaming Authority (MGA) is hereby declaring that it has no connection with 19msia.com. Any reference to the MGA and/or gaming licence/s said to be issued by the Maltese Authority, as stated by 19msia.com, are false and misleading.
